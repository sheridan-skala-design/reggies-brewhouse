Giving Back

More Than a Bar.

Reggie's is built into Owatonna. We host benefits, bake sales, and fundraisers for neighbors going through hard times — because when someone in this community needs help, we show up.

❤️
Benefit Nights
Fundraisers for families facing medical bills, emergencies, and unexpected hardships. Pull up, raise a glass, raise a few bucks.
🧁
Bake Sales
Community bake sales right at the bar. Sweet treats, good company, and every dollar goes back to the cause.
🤝
Local Fundraisers
Live music nights, raffles, and special events to support local schools, sports teams, and community organizations.
Have a Cause?
