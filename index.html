Entertainment

Something Going On
Every Week.

Live bands on weekends, leagues during the week, and the kind of energy that keeps you coming back. Reggie's is where Owatonna comes together.

🎸
Live Bands — Friday & Saturday
Local and touring bands hitting the stage every weekend. Rock, country, and everything in between.
🎱
Pool League
Weekly pool league on our tables. Competitive but friendly — all skill levels welcome.
🎯
Dart League
Steel tip dart league running weekly. Sign up at the bar or just show up.

Reggie's shuts down the block for a night of live music, drinks, and downtown Owatonna at its best.
